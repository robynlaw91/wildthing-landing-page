2. Age Requirements

IMPORTANT: Our services are strictly for adults aged 18 years and over only.

By making a booking, you confirm that:

  • You are at least 18 years of age

  • No minors will be present during the performance

If the performer arrives and discovers that minors are present, they reserve the right to cancel the performance without refund. The full booking fee will remain payable.

3. Booking Process

3.1 Making a Booking

Bookings can be made via our website, phone, or email. A booking is not confirmed until:

  • We have received and acknowledged your booking request
  • You have paid the required deposit
  • We have sent you written confirmation of your booking

3.2 Booking Information

When making a booking, you must provide accurate and complete information including:

  • Event date and time
  • Full venue address and postcode
  • Contact name and phone number for the day
  • Approximate number of guests
  • Any special requirements or requests

3.3 Performer Selection

While we endeavour to accommodate performer preferences, we cannot guarantee a specific performer. We reserve the right to substitute performers of equal quality and experience if necessary due to illness, emergency, or other unforeseen circumstances.

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Deposit

A non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total booking fee is required to secure your booking. The deposit must be paid at the time of booking.

4.2 Balance Payment

The remaining balance is due no later than 7 days before the event date. Failure to pay the balance may result in cancellation of the booking.

4.3 Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Visa and Mastercard (credit and debit)
  • American Express
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfer (BACS)

4.4 Travel Costs

Travel costs are calculated based on the distance from the nearest available performer to your venue. Travel costs are quoted at the time of booking and are additional to the package price. Travel costs are non-refundable once the performer has departed for your venue.

4.5 Prices

All prices quoted are in GBP (£) and include VAT where applicable. Prices are subject to change without notice, but changes will not affect bookings already confirmed with a deposit.

5. Cancellation Policy

5.1 Cancellation by Client

If you need to cancel your booking, the following refund policy applies:

  • 14+ days before event: Full refund minus £25 administration fee
  • 7-13 days before event: 50% refund of total booking fee
  • Less than 7 days before event: No refund

5.2 Rescheduling

We offer free rescheduling for bookings moved to a new date within 3 months of the original date, subject to availability. Rescheduling requests made less than 7 days before the event will incur a £50 rescheduling fee.

5.3 Cancellation by Us

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel your booking (e.g., due to performer illness with no available substitute), we will offer a full refund or the option to reschedule at no additional cost.

5.4 Force Majeure

We shall not be liable for any failure or delay in performing our obligations where such failure or delay results from circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to severe weather, natural disasters, pandemics, government restrictions, or acts of terrorism.

6. Performer Conduct

6.1 Professional Standards

All our performers are professional entertainers who adhere to strict standards of conduct. Performances are choreographed, tasteful, and designed to create a fun, memorable experience.

6.2 Boundaries

Our performers have the right to:

  • Set and maintain personal boundaries during the performance
  • End the performance early if they feel unsafe or are treated inappropriately
  • Decline requests that fall outside the agreed performance
  • Refuse to perform if guests appear heavily intoxicated or aggressive

6.3 Inappropriate Behaviour

If a performance is terminated early due to inappropriate behaviour by guests, no refund will be provided and the full booking fee remains payable.

7. Client Responsibilities

As the client, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring all guests are 18 years or older
  • Providing accurate venue and contact information
  • Ensuring the performer can access the venue
  • Providing a private space for changing if required
  • Ensuring guests treat the performer with respect
  • Obtaining any necessary permissions from venue owners
  • Being present or having a designated point of contact at the venue

8. Venue Requirements

The venue must meet the following requirements:

  • Be a private location (performances are not conducted in public spaces)
  • Have adequate space for the performance (minimum 3m x 3m clear floor space)
  • Have a private area for the performer to change
  • Be accessible for the performer (ground floor or lift access preferred)
  • Allow adult entertainment (some venues/landlords prohibit this)

We can perform at private residences, holiday accommodations, hotels (with permission), function rooms, and boats/yachts. Please inform us if the venue has any specific rules or access requirements.
